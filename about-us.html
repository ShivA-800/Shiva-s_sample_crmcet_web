About Us

One of the best College in Hyderabad

CMR College of Engineering & Technology (CMRCET for short) is sponsored by the MGR Educational Society, which was established in 2002. The College is situated 20 Km away from Secunderabad Railway Station on the Hyderabad – Nagpur National Highway (Medchal Road), Telangana, India. It is located in 10 acres of serene, lush green and pollution free environment. The management is committed in assuring quality service to all its stake holders viz parents, students, alumni, employees, employers and the community. Commitment and dedication are merged into our policy of continual quality improvement by establishing and implementing mechanisms and modalities, ensuring accountability at all levels, transparency in procedures and access to information and services.
